FAQ

Can I play CTGP on Dolphin?

  1. Short answer: No. CTGP intentionally doesn’t work on Dolphin and never will.
  2. Long answer: Kind of. You can create an ISO of the previous major version, CTGP Revolution v1.02, but it’s very old (last version was released in September 2012). There have been security holes that allow playing CTGP-R v1.03 on Dolphin, but these have been patched and you can’t use online features anymore.

How to play Mario Kart Wii on Wiimmfi using Dolphin Emulator

To play on Dolphin, you’ll need a few things:

  1. A backup of Mario Kart Wii
  2. A backup of a real Wii NAND
  3. A computer fast enough to play online smoothly

2. Rip Mario Kart Wii

This guide does not condone piracy. Go off the beaten path at your own risk.

You can use CleanRip to rip your Mario Kart Wii disc. You’ll need the Homebrew Channel for this. Softmod your Wii using wii.guide if you haven’t already.

3. Rip your Wii NAND

As stated earlier, you need homebrew. Use BootMii to backup your Wii NAND.

4. Configure Dolphin

Import your NAND dump into Dolphin and put the console MAC address in Dolphin configuration.

5. Patch Mario Kart Wii

Use the Wiimmfi patcher to patch your Mario Kart Wii backup.

Done!

Make sure Dolphin runs at full speed. If not, you may get banned.
